Core Services

Structural Openings

Specialists in major structural alterations, RSJs, load-bearing wall removal, basement structural work and forming new architectural spaces in homes, restaurants and commercial buildings.

Extensions

Rear, side and multi-storey extensions across London, Bromley, Sevenoaks and Kent — designed for modern open-plan living, natural light and increased property value.

Kitchen Renovations

High-end kitchen design, installation, underfloor heating, open-plan knock-throughs, bespoke cabinetry and architectural glazing integration.

Full Property Refurbishment

Complete luxury home transformations, reconfigurations, new bathrooms, flooring, re-wiring, plastering, rendering and premium finishes.

Landscaping

Porcelain paving, hard landscaping, sleeper walls, garden redesign, outdoor lighting and premium external upgrades.

Commercial Fit-Outs

Complete CAT A & CAT B fit-outs for offices, hospitality venues, retail spaces and commercial landlords across Central London and the South East.

Restaurant Refurbishments

Turnkey W1 hospitality projects including strip-outs, kitchen relocations, MEP upgrades, fire stopping, flooring, bar areas, and full back-of-house rebuilds.

Shopfitting

Precision joinery, interior transformations and branded shopfitting for restaurants, bars, cafés, retail and commercial spaces.
